About me

I am a driven Junior Technical Implementation Specialist at Printbox, where I bridge the gap between complex technical solutions and client needs. My daily work involves troubleshooting, configuring systems, and ensuring seamless software delivery.


Currently, I am finalizing my Master’s degree in Cybersecurity, which gives me a strong foundation in secure coding practices, data protection, and system analysis. I combine this academic knowledge with practical experience in C# (.NET) and Unity development.
